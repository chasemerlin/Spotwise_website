What We Do

Monitor Lots in Real-time

Our camera-based intelligent detection system utilizes a proprietary computer vision algorithm to detect and record vehicles in off-street lots and curbside parking zones. Our cameras know:

  • Which spots are occupied
  • How long each car has been there

Our system also integrates with billing data to determine:

  • Which spots are in violation
  • How long those spots have been in violation
  • Which spots will be in violation soon


Make Use of Your Data

Smart Enforcement Routing

Our software employs machine learning algorithms that put this new occupancy data to work. With this information, our system can reroute enforcement officers to the lots that make the most sense at the time, boosting efficiency along with revenue. This decision is based on:

  • Number of violators and soon to be violators
  • Distance to lot
  • Historical success rate at collecting ticket revenues in each lot
  • Other factors manually included by operator

Dynamic Pricing

The real-time occupancy data that our cameras relay can also be used to setup a dynamic pricing model. If your lot is filling up quickly, the Spotwise modeling algorithm can automatically switch the pricing tiers displayed on the pay stations to ensure you are charging the perfect amount to maximize your return.
